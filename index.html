About Me

Bio

Matthew Holmes is a full stack developer specializing in Python with a Bachelors of Science in Economics (focused in Management) from Oregon State University.

Matthew completed this degree program and his software training while working full time and taking a full course load. During this time he worked in the insurance and financial services industry as a licensed agent for AAA Oregon Idaho. There he learned principals of risk analysis, how to deliver an exceptional customer and end user experience and how to work in a high stress, heavily regulated environment....

In addition to his technical experience Matthew is an artist and a lover of all things nerdy. He learned to draw from studying D&D books and has been developing his passion for problem solving since childhood, playing Clue Finders on his Windows 95.

Matthew is a lifelong learner who has a passion for mentoring others and helping introverts like himself embrace their strengths through his website (nerd practitioner). Matthew is currently seeking employment as a mid level full stack software engineer and is focused on finding a diverse team that values collaboration, mentor-ship, continuous improvement and putting others first.
